All Is True

Loving you is the stupidest thing that I’ve ever done.
Loving you is the easiest thing that I’ve ever done.
Loving you is the most life-changing thing that I’ve ever done.
All is true.
Losing you is the stupidest thing that I’ve ever done.
Losing you is the hardest thing that I’ve ever done.
Losing you is the most life-changing thing that I’ve ever done.
All is true.
All, I would do again.
